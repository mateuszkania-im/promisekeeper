# PromiseKeeper Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2026

Controller: Mcat.one / Mateusz Kania
Contact: support@mcat.one

## 1. Scope

PromiseKeeper helps families manage daily promises and reminders. This policy explains what data is processed, why it is processed, and how users can contact us.

## 2. Data processed in current release

- Device-local profile data (parent name, children names/colors)
- Promises and statuses (pending/kept/moved), due dates and notes
- Local reminder settings (time and enabled state)
- Premium state in local placeholder mode until production billing is enabled

## 3. Data location

PromiseKeeper currently stores core data on the user device via local app storage. No account registration is required for core use.

## 4. Notifications

Daily reminders use system notification permissions. Notification schedule data is stored in-app.

## 5. Subscriptions

Paid plans are displayed in-app. For App Store billing, purchase processing is handled by Apple and subscription providers used by the app in production configuration.

## 6. What we do not require in current build

- No mandatory account signup for core flow
- No contact list import
- No GPS location requirement
- No camera access

## 7. User rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request information or deletion support by emailing support@mcat.one.

## 8. Policy updates

We may update this policy. Updated versions are published on this page.
